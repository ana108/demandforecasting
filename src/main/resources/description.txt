This app provides you with a list of popular formulas to use when forecasting demand.
Each formula has a name and a short description. To get a prediction of what your next demand value
will be, enter all your data, separated by either single space or coma and hit predict. The result should
be an approximate value of the demand in the next period.

It is important to pick a formula that is right for you data. Come back in a few weeks and maybe there
will be a tool to help you decide which formula works best for you.
